Dataset Information

recipe_id: id of the recipe
recipe_name: name of recipe
prep_time_minutes: time to prepare in minutes
categories_json: categories of the recipe as a json. ["category","categorey",...]. can be 
Categories_json: breakfast, lunch, dinner, meal(could be breakfast or dinner), dessert, snack, meat, dairy, vegetarian, vegan
star_rating: 1-5 if from a website or dataset, 6 if given by user. 
calories: calories per serving; nutrient_id: 1008
protein_g: protein in grams per serving; nutrient_id: 1003
carbs_g: carbs in grams per serving; nutrient_id: 1005
fat_g: fat in grams per serving; nutrient_id: 1004
fiber_g: fiber in grams per serving; nutrient_id: 1079
sodium_mg: sodium in miligrams per serving; nutrient_id: 1093 
instructions: instructions for recipe. 
ingredients_json: ingredients as a json file. ["ingredient":amount in grams, ....]
servings: must be 4
source: either user or url to websitem
